The capital of Illinois and the home of Abraham Lincoln.

Springfield is the capital of Illinois and the late home of Abraham Lincoln, where he resided for almost 30 years. Springfield has a very long history and is now where the Government of the state of Illinois resides.










Springfield is an urban city located in central Illinois and holds a population of around 115,000 people, making it the largest city in central Illinois. This city, the capital of Illinois was incorporated in the year 1821 and has the average income of about $81,000.
