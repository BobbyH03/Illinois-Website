My hometown, originally known as Fort Clark

Peoria is located right on the Illinois river which makes it a major trading and shipping center for Illinois. This also makes Peoria a very fishing friendly area with families enjoying time on the river.

Peoria has a population of about 113,000 people as of the 2020 census. This city was incorporated in the year 1680, making it one of the oldest cities in Peoria. Peoria is a urban city located in the central region of Illinois with an average income of about $80,000.
